    About Us

    Founded in 2020, IRAPSO was conceptualized by a group of three young and like-minded individuals, powered with the relentless passion and vision of enabling business and policy ecosystems through user-centric approaches, products and tools.

    Our Vision

    Our aim is to catalyse the holistic performance of organizations through value-oriented approaches and bespoke strategies, thereby creating incremental and sustainable impact in the developmental sector.

    Our Mission

    Our quest is to become a one-stop service provider of innovative products, tools and services in public policy, marketing and business development, enabling agile transformation of organisations.

    Our Values

    A team of curious and action-oriented individuals form the core of Irapso. Our values are driven by continually pushing the frontiers of knowledge, embracing diverse experiences and perspectives.

    PUBLIC POLICY

    What

    Our understanding of public policy is derived by discerning the complex social, political, economic and institutional factors vital to effectively analyse decisions and strategic initiatives, and to make meaningful, informed policy recommendations.

    Why

    Public policy is an amalgamation of formulation, analysis and authorisation process. Organisations working across the broad spectrum of the development sector often lack the vision of a multidisciplinary approach, which creates a gap between the policy interventions, implementation and outreach.

    How

    We will support organisations to become the trailblazers in enabling a robust policy and regulatory ecosystem. Using practical and adaptive frameworks, we intend to empower the organisations to engage in latest policy developments on the things that matter and apply the innovative suite of tools to the programs and decision making processes.

    Our Service Offerings

    Research and Analysis
    • Landscape and sectoral analysis
    • Policy and market scoping
    • Policy benchmarking
    Policy Advocacy
    • Stakeholder mapping and engagement plan
    • Advocacy Strategy, Communication plan and collaterals
    • Thought leadership through policy briefs and reports

    MARKETING

    What

    In the development sector, the notion of marketing is underpinned by a narrative and story-telling with a consumer centric approach. With this understanding, we aim to develop result-oriented marketing strategies in alignment with the organisational goals of creating social impact and strengthening brand identity in the public discourse.

    Why

    The biggest challenges organisations in the development space face, when it comes to marketing, are targeting and positioning. Organisations often have to juggle between the duality of these concepts and apply the most appropriate solution in line with their present needs and requirements.

    How

    We will identify, via market research, the ideal targeting and positioning strategy for the organisations. Our uniqueness comes with the ability to conceptualize core challenges and offer market insights relevant to the multidimensional development sector and therefore apply tailored approaches to perceive a solution. We will prepare and execute offline and online advertising and fundraising strategies catering to their short term and long term requirements as a one-time service or a steady flow of the product or service.

    Our Service Offerings

    Digital Marketing
    • Enhancing the brand presence on social media
    • Marketing trends and analytics
    • Digital Marketing documents and collaterals
    Fundraising
    • Offline promotion activities through traditional media outlets
    • Organise networking and fundraising events and support fundraising through online platforms
    • Tie-ups with donor agencies and development sector partners for fundraising activities

    BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

    What

    The journey of converting business prospects into successful leads requires sustained efforts combined with systematic planning. A concise understanding of organizational needs in line with the short term and long term goals is therefore of paramount importance in devising a comprehensive strategy. We are here to support organizations in their quest to frame a purpose driven approach to business development.

    Why

    Crafting a successful pitch requires a deep seeded understanding of complex developmental challenges and practical solutions. While working on multiple engagements, organizations oftentimes are constricted with the limited availability of human resources and the technical know-how of drafting a sound proposal in a limited time frame.

    How

    Using insights and intelligence, we help organisations devise strategic action plans in writing compelling proposals. Be it a call for proposal following a set pattern or a concept note based on a series of brainstorming sessions, we are here to help prepare a response detailing the key ideas of implementation. We can also help leverage community connections through networking platforms and specialised services.

    Our Service Offerings

    Tender Tracking
    • Opportunity identification
    • Mapping of eligibility requirements to the client needs
    • Proposal research and writing
    Foster Partnerships
    • Business collaborations
    • Knowledge management and documentation
    • Stretagic and need-oriented tools database

    Team

    Our journey started in the summer of 2020. This unprecedented situation proved the age-old adage “Change is the only constant”. While we were confined behind closed doors of our homes, our thoughts weren’t.

    The testing times brought together three young professionals(read friends) from different walks of life. They discussed and brainstormed on random and not-so-random ideas- how the ‘new normal’ has affected their workspaces, their lives; whether organisations are prepared to adjust their working styles and operations in these circumstances. Such thought provoking sessions tingled their minds in seeing things differently, as they decided to venture into the space combining individual capabilities. Thus came the Eureka moment for IRAPSO.

    With their professional experience, the founding members have been inspired by the selfless work done by people and organisations across the globe. IRAPSO’s sole focus is to create an ecosystem which enhances the visibility and operations of the non-profit organizations and other actors in the development landscape.
